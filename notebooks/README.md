## 01 — CAZ Science
[![Open In Colab](https://colab.research.google.com/assets/colab-badge.svg)](https://colab.research.google.com/github/jamesrahenry/cia-demo/blob/main/notebooks/01_caz_science.ipynb)

Loads a language model and runs a full CAZ analysis on any concept you choose. Shows where the concept crystallises layer by layer, whether allocation is multimodal, and how your concept compares to the 17 paper concepts. No setup required — runs on a free Colab T4.
